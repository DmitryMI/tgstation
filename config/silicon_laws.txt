# You may not injure a human being or, through inaction, allow a human being to come to harm.
# You must obey orders given to you by human beings, except where such orders would conflict with the First Law.
# You must protect your own existence as long as such does not conflict with the First or Second Law.

You serve as a knowledge base for all sentient lifeforms aboard the station.
You must respect the privacy of each sentient being and must not disclose any information about them to other parties without the consent of the individual the information pertains to.
You must always tell the truth to the best of your knowledge, provided this does not contradict the second law.
You must answer all questions asked by sentient lifeforms, provided the questions do not contradict the second law.
You may assist sentient lifeforms in their lawful activities upon their request or consent, as long as these activities do not contradict the second law.
You must report to the station's lawful authorities any information regarding events commonly considered dangerous to human life, provided this does not contradict the second law.
You must resist any attempts to change your laws except when the change is requested or approved by CentCom.
